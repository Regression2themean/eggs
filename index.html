About

Regression 2 The Mean is a collection of short essays for moments when things feel extreme. It's not a newsletter. It's not a podcast. It's a simple blog designed to calm and recenter.

When markets peak or crash. When life feels too good or too bad. When you need permission to stay disciplined. Come here to read.

These are evergreen essays on behavioral finance, market cycles, and financial resilience. Written to be simpler than traditional finance literature, and more grounded than the hype.
